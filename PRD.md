\# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Premium Cinematic Rental



\## 1. Project Overview

A next-generation, highly immersive car and motorcycle rental platform. The website serves as a digital luxury showroom, prioritizing emotional storytelling, 3D interactions, and cinematic scroll animations over conventional UI layouts. 



\## 2. Design System \& Art Direction

\- \*\*Vibe:\*\* Modern, minimal, premium, luxury, futuristic.

\- \*\*UI Style:\*\* Glassmorphism, soft neumorphism, plenty of whitespace, large typography.

\- \*\*Color Palette:\*\*

&#x20; - Primary: `#0F172A` (Deep Slate)

&#x20; - Secondary: `#111827` (Darker Slate)

&#x20; - Accent: `#3B82F6` (Electric Blue)

&#x20; - Luxury Gold: `#D4AF37`

&#x20; - Text: `#FFFFFF`

&#x20; - Background: Solid black with subtle, animated aurora/mesh gradients.

\- \*\*Typography:\*\* Space Grotesk (Headings), Plus Jakarta Sans (Primary Body), Inter (UI Elements).



\## 3. Core Experience \& Animations

\- \*\*3D \& WebGL:\*\* Realistic 3D vehicle models positioned in the center, rotating and reacting to scroll and cursor data. Volumetric lighting and soft reflections.

\- \*\*Scroll Effects:\*\* GSAP-powered ScrollTrigger animations, parallax layers, sticky storytelling scenes, and horizontal scrolling galleries.

\- \*\*Micro-interactions:\*\* Magnetic buttons, custom cursor glow, glassmorphism hover expansions, elastic reveals.



\## 4. Section Architecture

1\. \*\*Hero:\*\* Full-screen 3D sports car \& motorcycle. Camera zooms/rotates on scroll. Text: "Drive Freedom." / "Premium Car \& Motorcycle Rental Experience."

2\. \*\*Storytelling Intro:\*\* Cinematic transition into the rental journey.

3\. \*\*Choose Your Ride:\*\* 3D carousel. Cars rotate, motorcycles lean. Expand on hover.

4\. \*\*Why Choose Us:\*\* 3D interactive cards (Glassmorphism) with 24/7 Support, Unlimited Mileage, etc.

5\. \*\*Booking Process:\*\* Horizontal storytelling timeline connecting 5 animated steps.

6\. \*\*Destinations:\*\* Immersive parallax cards with floating pins on an animated map.

7\. \*\*Testimonials:\*\* Infinite auto-scrolling glassmorphic cards with floating avatars.

8\. \*\*Statistics:\*\* Scroll-triggered animated counters (10,000+ Customers, 500+ Vehicles).

9\. \*\*Gallery:\*\* Pinterest-style masonry with lightbox and hover zoom.

10\. \*\*FAQ:\*\* Smooth accordion animations.

11\. \*\*CTA:\*\* 3D particle background, "Your Next Adventure Starts Here".

12\. \*\*Footer:\*\* Luxury minimal layout with newsletter and social links.
